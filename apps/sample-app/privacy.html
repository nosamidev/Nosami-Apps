Privacy Policy

App: Café POS  ·  Last updated: June 2025

Nosami Apps ("we", "us", or "our") built Café POS as a free/paid Android application available on Google Play. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of this app.

By using this app, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you disagree, please discontinue use of the app.

1. Data We Collect

Café POS is fully offline. The app does not transmit any data to external servers. All information you enter (products, orders, shifts, receipts) is stored exclusively on your device using a local database.

We do not collect:

  • Personal identifiable information
  • Location data
  • Payment or financial information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs

2. Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services. No data is shared with third parties.

3. Permissions Used

  • USB Host — required to communicate with the thermal receipt printer

No permissions are used beyond what is strictly necessary for the app's core functionality.

4. Data Storage & Security

All app data is stored locally on your device using Android's built-in SQLite database. Data is accessible only to the app and is protected by Android's standard app sandboxing. We recommend using device-level security (PIN, fingerprint) to protect your data.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Data is retained as long as the app is installed on your device. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data. You can also clear app data manually via Android Settings → Apps → Café POS → Clear Data.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
