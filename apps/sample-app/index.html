About this app

Café POS is a lightweight, offline-first point-of-sale application built for small cafés and food businesses. Designed to work without an internet connection, it keeps your service running even when connectivity is unavailable.

Built for Android and optimized for dedicated POS terminals, Café POS handles your full order flow — from taking orders to printing thermal receipts.

Features

  • Full offline operation — no internet required
  • Product grid with categories (Breakfast, Drinks, Popular, Extras)
  • Shift management with clock-in / clock-out per server
  • Thermal receipt printing via USB (ESC/POS)
  • Order history and ticket management
  • Admin panel for menu and settings

Compatibility

Requires Android 5.1 (API level 22) or higher. Optimized for dedicated Android POS terminals. Works on standard Android phones and tablets.

Privacy

This app operates fully offline. No personal data is transmitted to external servers. All data (orders, products, shifts) is stored locally on your device. Read the full Privacy Policy →
