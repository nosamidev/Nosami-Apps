Privacy Policy

Applies to all Nosami Apps  ·  Last updated: June 2025

Nosami Apps ("we", "us", or "our") develops Android applications available on Google Play. This Privacy Policy describes our general data practices across all our apps. Each app may also have its own privacy page with app-specific details.

1. Information We Collect

We design our apps to collect as little data as possible. What is collected, if anything, depends on the specific app. General categories include:

  • Usage data (features used, session duration) — only where stated in the app's own privacy page
  • Device info (OS version, language) — for compatibility and crash debugging only
  • Crash reports — to improve app stability, via opt-in or third-party services listed per app

Apps that operate fully offline collect no data whatsoever. Refer to each app's individual Privacy Policy for specifics.

2. How We Use Information

Any information collected is used solely to:

  • Operate and improve the app
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Respond to support requests

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Some apps may integrate third-party services (e.g., Google Play Services, Firebase). These services operate under their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase

Each app's individual privacy page will list exactly which services are used for that app.

4. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable measures to protect any data processed. Locally stored data is protected by Android's standard app sandboxing model. No security system is 100% guaranteed, but we take appropriate precautions.

5. Children's Privacy

None of our apps are directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Contact us if you believe a child has submitted personal information.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of any data we hold about you
  • Ask what data is collected by a specific app
  • Opt out of any optional analytics

To exercise these rights, email us at the address below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes take effect when posted on this page. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
