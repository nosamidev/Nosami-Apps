Privacy Policy

Draw Together: Drawing Game for Two · Developer: NosamiDev · Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Draw Together: Drawing Game for Two ("the App"), published by NosamiDev ("we", "us", or "our"), collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the App.

This Privacy Policy applies specifically to the Android application Draw Together: Drawing Game for Two available on Google Play.

1. Information We Collect

Anonymous User Identifier

The App uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication to create a unique anonymous identifier for each app installation or user session. This identifier is used only to provide app functionality and does not directly identify you.

Nickname

The App allows users to choose a nickname. This nickname may be visible to the other participant inside the same drawing room.

User-Generated Content

The App stores user-generated drawing content and room information so that two participants can draw together on the same shared canvas.

  • Drawings and strokes created in a room
  • Room codes and room information
  • Shared canvas data

Presence Information

The App may temporarily store connection or presence information, such as whether a participant is online inside a drawing room.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information only to:

  • Create and manage private drawing rooms
  • Synchronize drawings between two participants
  • Display nicknames inside shared rooms
  • Maintain room status and connection status
  • Provide the core functionality of the App

We do not sell, rent, or use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Firebase

The App uses Google Firebase services, including Firebase Authentication and Firebase Realtime Database, to provide anonymous sign-in and real-time drawing synchronization.

Data may be processed and stored on Google's Firebase servers according to Google's privacy and security practices.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy and Security

4. Data Retention

Drawing rooms are temporary. Expired rooms and related drawing data are deleted automatically after the retention period configured by the App.

Images saved by users to their gallery remain on the user's own device and are not uploaded by the App as separate image files.

5. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect:

  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Physical addresses
  • Payment information
  • Contacts
  • Precise location data
  • Camera data
  • Microphone recordings
  • Government identifiers

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal information with advertisers, marketing companies, or data brokers.

Information may be processed by trusted service providers required for app operation, including Google Firebase Authentication and Google Firebase Realtime Database.

7. Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect information processed by the App. Data transmitted between the App and Firebase services is encrypted in transit using industry-standard security protocols.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age required by applicable local laws without parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable laws.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, or deletion of information associated with your use of the App.

To request deletion or ask questions about your data, contact us using the email address below.
