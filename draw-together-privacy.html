Privacy Policy

Draw Together: Drawing Game for Two · Developer: NosamiDev · Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy applies only to the Android application Draw Together: Drawing Game for Two published by NosamiDev. It explains what information the app uses, how that information is used, and what the app does not collect.

App-specific notice: This page is for Draw Together: Drawing Game for Two only. It does not describe privacy practices for other Nosami apps.

1. Information the App Uses

The app uses the following information and services to make the multiplayer drawing experience work:

  • Firebase Anonymous Authentication to create an anonymous account identifier for app use
  • Firebase Realtime Database to sync room activity between players in real time
  • Nickname chosen by the user for display inside a room
  • Drawing room data such as room codes, room state, and participant session data
  • User-generated drawing content including strokes, lines, and shared canvas updates
  • Presence / online status to show whether a participant is connected in a room

The anonymous Firebase identifier is used only to support app functionality. It is not an email address, phone number, or real-name account.

2. How the Information Is Used

The app uses this information only to provide its core features, including:

  • Creating and joining shared drawing rooms
  • Showing nicknames to the two participants in a room
  • Synchronizing drawings in real time between players
  • Maintaining room activity and connection status
  • Keeping the shared drawing session functional while the room is active

This data is used only for app functionality. It is not sold and is not used for advertising purposes.

3. What the App Does Not Collect

Draw Together: Drawing Game for Two does not collect the following:

  • Email
  • Phone number
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Payment information

4. Third-Party Services

The app relies on Google Firebase services to operate. In particular, it uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication and Firebase Realtime Database. Information processed through these services may be handled on Google's systems according to Google's policies.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy and Security

5. Data Retention

Room data, drawing content, and presence information may be stored temporarily for the duration of active or recent sessions so the app can function properly. Data may be deleted when rooms expire, are cleared, or are no longer needed for app operation.

Images saved by users remain on the user's own device. Saved images are not uploaded by the app as separate image files.

6. Data Sharing

NosamiDev does not sell personal data. Information used by the app is not shared with advertisers or data brokers. Data is processed only as needed to operate the app, including through Firebase services.

7. Security

Reasonable measures are used to protect information processed by the app. Data sent between the app and Firebase services is transmitted over secure connections.

8. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app uses anonymous access and simple room-based gameplay, users should still use the app responsibly and under appropriate supervision where required.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
